Easy Payment Plan

-

SUNROOOF Installation happens in 6 phases:

Stage 1 (Booking):

The order is booked once you clear the 1st Payment Milestone, we arrange a site visit for accurate measurements.

Stage 2 (Production):

We prepare Shop Drawings and seek your approval before production begins. Once we get the approval & 2nd Payment Milestone is cleared, production begins and we manufacture the wooden frames for your site.

Stage 3 (Wooden Frames):

Once ready for dispatch, you clear the 3rd Payment Milestone & then we dispatch and install the wooden frames at the site.

Stage 4 (Your Turn):

Next, it's your turn! You then pack the ceiling / wall with false ceiling / gypsum boards, do all your paint work and all other dust related work.

Stage 5 (Light Consoles):

Once the site is dust-free, you clear the 4th Payment Milestone & then we dispatch and install the light consoles at the site.

Stage 6 (Handover):

After light consoles are installed, we setup the automation & handover the project.
